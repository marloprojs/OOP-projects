What is SDNP?

This is an online app that allows you to get new music playlists based off of your music preferences, the more filters you apply to a specific playlist, the more uniquely tuned the new playlist will be. This is a playlist creation system solely based off of what filters you put on a specific playlist.

How does it work?

You can create new playlists for every mood! just select a playlist and filter, and name it!
